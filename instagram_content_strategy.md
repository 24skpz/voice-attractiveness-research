## SECTION 4: FORMAT PERFORMANCE RANKING

For this product specifically, here is how each format ranks by business impact:

| Rank | Format | Why |
|------|--------|-----|
| 1 | Reels | Highest reach, algorithm boost, best for top-of-funnel discovery |
| 2 | Stories (with link sticker) | Highest direct conversion to site visits |
| 3 | Carousel Posts | Highest save rate, best for authority building |
| 4 | Single Image Posts | Lowest effort, good for maintaining posting frequency |

**Recommended weekly cadence:**
- 3 Reels per week (Tue, Thu, Sat)
- Daily Stories (mix of polls, facts, score reveals)
- 2 Feed posts per week (Tue, Thu -- stagger with Reels)
